Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan my crops for diseases?

To scan your crops, go to the "Crop Scan" section and either upload an image of your crops or use the camera feature to capture images directly. The system will analyze the image and provide a detailed report on any detected diseases or pests.

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our AI-powered disease detection system has an accuracy rate of approximately 92% for common crop diseases. Accuracy may vary based on image quality and the specific disease. We recommend taking clear, well-lit photos of affected leaves for best results.

What crops are supported by Kri-shak?

Kri-shak currently supports major crops including wheat, rice, cotton, sugarcane, corn, and soybeans. We are continuously expanding our database to include more crops. If your crop is not yet supported, please contact us with your request.

How do I interpret the pesticide recommendations?

The pesticide recommendations are tailored based on the specific disease detected, infection severity, and crop type. Each recommendation includes dosage instructions, application frequency, and safety precautions. Always follow label instructions and wear protective equipment when applying pesticides.

Can I use Kri-shak on mobile devices?

Yes, Kri-shak is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. You can access all features including crop scanning, disease detection, and pesticide recommendations from your smartphone or tablet. We also recommend downloading our mobile app for the best experience.
